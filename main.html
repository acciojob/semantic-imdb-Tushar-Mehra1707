Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a drama film about Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongfully convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. He is sentenced to life in Shawshank prison, where he befriends Ellis "Red" Redding. Over the years, Andy uses his intelligence and skills to gain the respect of his fellow inmates and create a plan for eventual escape, showing hope and perseverance in the face of adversity.

Actors

Tim Robbins plays Andy Dufresne, the main protagonist, a man wrongly convicted of a crime.

Morgan Freeman plays Ellis "Red" Redding, a long-time inmate who becomes Andy's close friend and mentor.

Bob Gunton plays Warden Samuel Norton, the corrupt warden of Shawshank prison.

Favorite Quote

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." — Andy Dufresne

This quote embodies the core theme of the film — choosing hope over despair and taking control of your own fate.
